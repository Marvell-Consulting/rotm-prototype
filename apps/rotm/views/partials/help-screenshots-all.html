Help me take a screenshot

How to take a screenshot on a desktop computer

  • Mac
  • Chromebook
  • All other compters

Try this:

  • Press the Shift key, command (or Cmd), and 3
  • The screenshot will be saved to your Desktop
  • You can now upload it to the form

Get more help from Apple

Try this:

  • Press the Ctrl key and the switch window key
  • The screenshot will be saved to your Downloads
  • You can now upload it to the form

If that doesn’t work, try pressing Ctrl and F5.

Get more help from Android Central

Try this:

  • Press the Prt Scr key (or similar) to take a copy of your screen
  • Click into the box below
  • Press ctrl and V to paste the image into the box

If that doesn’t work, try one of thes:.

  • Pressing Prt Scr and Windows logo together
  • Pressing Prt Scr, Windows logo and Fn together
  • Pressing Prt Scr, Alt and Fn together

Then paste the image into the box below.

On some computers, the image may be saved to a folder called Screenshots in your Pictures.

How to take a screenshot on a mobile phone

  • iPhones
  • All other phones and tablets

How to take a screenshot on iPhone X or later

Press the side button and volume up at the exact same time, then quickly release. A thumbnail should flash up on your screen if it’s worked.

The screenshot will be saved to your photos.

How to take a screenshot on iPhone 8, 7, or 6

Press the side button and the home button at the exact same time, then quickly release. A thumbnail should flash up on your screen if it’s worked.

The screenshot will be saved to your photos.

How to take a screenshot on iPhone SE, 5, or earlier

Press the top button and the home button at the exact same time, then quickly release. A thumbnail should flash up on your screen if it’s worked.

The screenshot will be saved to your photos.

Get more help from Apple

Taking a photo of your screen on other phones and tablets

Press the power button and volume down at the exact same time, then quickly release.

The screenshot should be saved in your Gallery or Pictures.

If that doesn’t work, try:

  • Holding down the power button and the home button or home key on your phone’s screen
  • Holding down the power button and the volume up at the same time.
