Taking a photo of your screen on iPhones

Try one of these:

  • Press the side button and volume up at the same time. Quickly release both buttons.
  • Press the side button and the home button at the same time. Quickly release both buttons
  • Press the top button and the home button at the same time. Quickly release both buttons.

The screenshot will be saved to a folder called Screenshots in your Album.

Taking a photo of your screen on other phones and tablets

Try this:

  • Hold down the power button and volume down at the same time for a few seconds.

The screenshot should be saved in your Gallery or Pictures.
