Help me find the link

You can find the link or URL at the top of the page. It starts with facebook.com and might be followed by other words, letters or symbols. For example, facebook.com/settings?tab=blocking

To link to something someone has posted, click on the time of the post, for example ’18 hrs’ or ‘Yesterday at 06:52’. This will open the post and you can copy the link at the top of the page as normal.
