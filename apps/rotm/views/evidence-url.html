Help me find the link

On a website, the link is at the top of the page. It usually starts with the website name followed by a series of words, symbols or numbers. For example, the link to this page would be https://rotm-testing.herokuapp.com/evidence-url

In an app, there can be different ways of finding the link. Check the help pages in your app for more information.
